OUR STORY

A Passion For The Perfect Brew

Born from a deep love for artisan coffee, Ember & Roast was designed to be your sanctuary in the city. Since 2018, we have been obsessed with sourcing the finest organic beans from sustainable farms across the globe.

We believe every cup tells a story — of the soil it grew in, the hands that harvested it, and the barista who precisely crafted it for you. Come for the coffee, stay for the warmth.
